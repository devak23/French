it is = c'est (say)
is it = est-ce que c'est (es kuh say)
what = que (kuh)
what is it = que c'est (kes kuh say)
when = quand (kaun) [n in quand is nasal]
when is it = quand est-ce que c'est (kaunt es kuh say)
where = où (ooh)
where is it = où est-ce que c'est (ooh es kuh say) OR où est-il (ooh ee-teel)
There = là (laah)
There it is = Voilà

the wine = le vin (looh-vun) [n in vun is nasal]
I would like wine = je voudrais du vin (je voo-dray-duh doo-vun) [n in vun is nasal]
I would like some wine = je voudrais du vin (je voo-dray-duh doo-vun) [n in vun is nasal]
I would like the wine = je voudrais le vin (je voo-dray-le looh-vun) [n in vun is nasal]

the bread = la baguette (la ba-gay-tuh) / le pain (looh-pahn) [n in pain is nasal]
I would like bread = je voudrais du pain (je voo-dray-duh looh-pahn) [n in pain is nasal]
I would like some bread = je voudrais du pain (je voo-dray-duh looh-pahn) [n in pain is nasal]
I would like the bread = je voudrais le pain (je voo-dray-le looh-pahn) [n in pain is nasal]

the red wine = le vin rouge (looh-vun roo-zsh) [n in rouge is nasal]
I would like red wine and bread = je voudrais du vin rouge et du pain (je voo-dray-duh looh-vun roo-zsh et duh looh-pahn) [n in rouge and pain is nasal]

the wine leaf = le feuille de vin (looh-fyee duh vun) [n in vun is nasal]
